History

Our tech conference was established in 2015 with the vision of creating a platform where technology enthusiasts, professionals, and innovators could come together to exchange ideas, share knowledge, and explore emerging trends. What started as a small local gathering has grown into an internationally recognized event, drawing attendees from diverse industries and backgrounds. Over the years, the conference has featured groundbreaking talks, hands-on workshops, and inspiring keynotes from leaders in AI, software development, data science, and more. With a focus on collaboration and innovation, the event has become a hub for networking and launching transformative projects, shaping the future of technology one session at a time
